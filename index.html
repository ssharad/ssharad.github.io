I am currently working with the Centre for Networked Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India.

I completed my masters in Computer Science with a specialization in Data Science and Technology from the Web Information Systems group, at the Delft University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Dr. ir. Alessandro Bozzon. During my stay in the Netherlands, I spent the Summer of 2018 as an IBM Extreme Blue intern.

My current research interests are broadly in the topics of Human-centered AI, Natural Language Processing, Edge Intelligence, and Data-driven Modelling.

More about my research and resulting publications can be found in the research page.
