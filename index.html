About Me

I am at present pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at the Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands.

Prior to starting my master's program at TU Delft, my research as a student at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham in India was in the development of building smart ecosystems combining the Internet of Things with Predictive Analytics. The research projects focused on the energy and transportation sectors and can be found in the proceedings of ACM e-Energy, IEEE ANTS, and Procedia Computer Science.

After coming to TU Delft, my research interests have broadened to span across Crowd Computing, Data Science, Machine Learning along with the Internet of Things. During my masters, my research focuses using on Urban Data Science for Smart Cities with the Internet of Things and Crowd as data sources.

I either write or listen to music or binge watch animes and seldom take long walks to divert myself from studies and work.
