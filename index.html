Short Bio

At present, I am pursuing a full Masters programme in Computer Science at the Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands. Prior to this, I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering with first class from Amrita University in India.

My current reseach focus is to integrate intelligence with the data, leveraging the concepts of the Internet of Things and Data Science.
