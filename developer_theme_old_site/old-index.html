About Me

I hold a Masters in Computer Science degree from the Delft University of Technology with a specialization in Data Science and Technology. In my master thesis, I designed and developed a hybrid workflow which combined human intelligence (crowdsourcing) with deep learning models to detect and obfuscate private information in images used for image analysis crowdsourcing tasks. My current research interests broadly lie in the topics of edge intelligence - bringing machine intelligence to edge devices like IoT nodes, and human-in-the-loop AI. Recently, the topic of swarm intelligence and its application for societal problems have piqued my research interest. I am open to collaborating on these or a new research topic.
