About Me

I hold a Masters in Computer Science degree from the Delft University of Technology under the supervision of Alessandro Bozzon. My current research focus is to study how human and machine intelligence be combined to bring intelligence on Edge Devices like IoT nodes. Concurrently, I am also interested to study how distributed intelligence can be acheived in networked applications, swarm robotics.
