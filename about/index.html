About

I am currently working with the Centre for Networked Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India. Prior to my current position, I completed my masters in Computer Science (August 2019) with a specialization in Data Science and Technology from the Web Information Systems group, at the Delft University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Dr. ir. Alessandro Bozzon. I hold a B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering (2016) from Amrita University, Coimbatore. During my stay in the Netherlands, I worked as an Extreme Blue intern at IBM Netherlands BV in Amsterdam (June - September 2018). My research interests are broadly in the topics of edge intelligence, edge analytics, human-centered AI, and data-driven modelling. More on my research can be found on this page.
