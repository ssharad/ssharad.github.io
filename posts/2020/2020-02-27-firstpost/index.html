Hi!!

If you are stopping by this page, it’s really nice of you to drop by, Thank you!

It has been a while, like a few years, since I wanted to write and maintain a blog. I had spent close these few years thinking, scribbling and rethinking of what I would want to write about, while my vocabulary witnessed a gradual degradation in the context of an operating system.

In those four years, I had been building some awesome things, mostly as part of my courserwork but now that I think about them, I lost most of my notes working on them. Thus, this blog will become a sort of development journal where I shall write about my experiences and approach to setup the development environment and start working on my current projects.

I shall also revisit some of the older ones and post them as self-contained posts which you could try on your own computer.

The time frame between the first few posts should be fairly small and I shall try to update this blog periodically.

Till next time!
